Buffalo Chicken Dip


Description

Buffalo Chicken Dip is a creamy, spicy, and irresistible appetizer that has become a staple at parties, tailgates, and casual gatherings. It's typically made by combining shredded cooked chicken, rich cream cheese, a generous amount of sharp cheddar or mozzarella cheese, ranch or blue cheese dressing, and, most importantly, the namesake ingredient: spicy buffalo wing sauce. This mixture is baked until it is hot, bubbly, and easily scoopable, delivering the comforting, savory flavor of classic buffalo wings in a format perfect for sharing.

The dip is a textural and flavor powerhouse, offering a warm, velvety consistency from the melted cheeses and cream cheese, complemented by the tangy heat of the buffalo sauce. It's often served hot with a variety of dippers, such as sturdy tortilla chips, celery sticks, carrot sticks, or slices of toasted baguette. Because of its intense, familiar flavor and ease of preparation, Buffalo Chicken Dip is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser and a popular go-to recipe for any occasion requiring a delicious, high-impact snack.

Ingridients

  • Chicken
  • Cream Cheese
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Hot Sauce
  • Cheese

    • How to cook

    1. Gather all ingredients.
    2. Heat chicken and hot pepper sauce in a skillet over medium heat until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in cream cheese and ranch dressing. Cook and stir until well blended and warm, 3 to 5 minutes.
    3. Mix in 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese, and transfer the mixture to a slow cooker. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over top.
    4. Cover and cook on Low until dip is hot and bubbly, about 35 minutes.
    5. Serve with celery sticks and crackers.
